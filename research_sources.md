## About Apollo

### LM Info

- Mass (total): 15,200 - 16,400 kg (with rover)
- Mass:
    - Descent stage: 
        - gross: 10,334 kg
        - dry: 2,134 kg (approx.)
    - Ascent stage: 
        - gross: 4,700 kg
        - dry: 2,150 kg
- Descent Propulsion System (DPS -- The rocket)
    - mass: 179 kg
    - fuel mass: 8,200 kg
    - thrust: 45,040 N
        - Throttlable between 10 and 60%
    - Specific impulse: 311 s
    - Delta V: 2,500 m/s
